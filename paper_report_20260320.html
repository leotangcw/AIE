Beyond Muon: MUD (MomentUm Decorrelation) for Faster Transformer Training

⚙️ 优化器 🚀 训练加速

🎨 图像编辑 ✨ 无需训练

Efficient Training-Free Multi-Token Prediction via Embedding-Space Probing
